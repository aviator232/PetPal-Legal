Delete Account - PetPal

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your PetPal account and associated data.

Option 1: Delete Inside the App (Recommended)

  1. Open PetPal.
  2. Go to Account.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Type DELETE to confirm.

Option 2: Request by Email

If you cannot access the app, email: petpal.help1@gmail.com

Use the subject: Account Deletion Request and include the email address associated with your PetPal account.

What Gets Deleted

  • Account profile information
  • Pet profiles and related records
  • Reminders and schedules
  • Expense/pantry data and uploaded files associated with your account

Processing Time

We process deletion requests as quickly as possible after verification. Limited backup/log records may persist temporarily as described in our Privacy Policy.

Need help? Contact: petpal.help1@gmail.com
